# Privacy Policy for NexBeat

Last updated: June 4, 2026

NexBeat is built as an Open Source, community-driven application. This service is provided at no cost and is intended for use as-is.

### 1. Information Collection and Use
NexBeat does not collect, track, or share any personally identifiable information (PII). All user configurations, offline downloaded tracks, and caches are stored locally on your device.

### 2. Third-Party Services
To provide core playback and search features, NexBeat interacts directly with the following public endpoints:
- **YouTube Music API**: To stream music and search catalogs.
- **LrcLib, KuGou, and SimpMusic**: To fetch lyrics.
- **Shazam**: For in-app music recognition features.
- **Last.fm and ListenBrainz**: If you explicitly configure and enable scrobbling.
- **Discord**: If you enable Discord Rich Presence integration.
- **GitHub**: To check for and download update packages.

These external services are governed by their respective privacy policies. No data is sent to private or proprietary NexBeat servers.

### 3. Permissions Requested
- **Storage / Media Audio**: To read and play local audio files stored on your device.
- **Internet / Network State**: To stream online music, check for updates, and fetch lyrics.
- **Notifications**: To show playback controls in the status bar and notify you about new updates.

### 4. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us via our GitHub Repository: https://github.com/PandaSL2/NexBeat
